Textile Engineering Researcher

Tasneem Noor

I am a textile engineering researcher based in Dhaka, Bangladesh, with a B.Sc. from Ahsanullah University of Science & Technology and a growing portfolio of peer-reviewed publications across functional, biomedical, and sustainable textiles. My work centers on surface modification, bioactive finishes, natural dyes, and the application of textiles in healthcare — areas where material innovation meets real-world impact.

I have co-authored seven journal articles in outlets including ACS Omega, RSC Advances, and Biomedical Materials, and I currently contribute to production planning and R&D at Envoy Textiles Limited. I am actively seeking graduate research opportunities where I can deepen my investigation of advanced textile functionalization and sustainable material science.
